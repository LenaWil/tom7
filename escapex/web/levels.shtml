Escape has a built-in level editor and allows you to easily share your creations with other players through this server. This page allows you to browse through the level collection on the web, to see detailed information about the levels or to see what's new when you're away from the game.

Another way to browse levels is by looking at the list of most recent comments: most recent comments (including spoilers)

The list of new levels is also available as an RSS feed (every level or weekly summary ), which you can subscribe to with an RSS-enabled newsreader. There is even a livejournal user escape_feed to befriend. You can also become a fan of the Escape Facebook page.

To upload your own levels, follow these steps:

  1. Ensure your player is registered (press r on the main menu)
  2. Create a level, and save it in the "My Levels" directory. Exit the editor.
  3. Load your level and solve it. Your solution must be saved so that it can be verified by the server.
  4. Once you are happy with your level and have the yellow check mark, you can press ctrl-u to upload it. It will appear here instantly!
