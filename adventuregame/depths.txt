
Managing movieclips depths in flash is tricky.
This game uses certain rules about depths.

Anything that is visible must have a depth of 100 or greater. 
Its depth should be equal to its 

There are some reserved depths between 0 and 100:

 0 : never used, except temporarily in order to delete a
     movie clip

 99: the floor has this depth.

 98960-98969: foreground movieclips
 98970-98979: foreground effects
 99500: Menubar
 99980: player familiar
 99999: the border around the screen has this depth
 

other important stuff:

We universally use these codes to represent the
four directions:

1  right
2  left
3  down
4  up
